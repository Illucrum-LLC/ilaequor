FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything people ask before joining the development updates list. If your question is not here, you can ask us directly on szymon.kokot@illucrum.com.

Do I have to switch fertilizers to use Ilaequor?

No. Ilaequor works from the fertilizers you already dose. You enter each stock once, the fertilizer, the grams, and the volume of water you dissolved it in, and the app does the dosing math from your tank volume. There is nothing new to buy and no product line to switch to.

Which dosing methods does it support: only GEN, or also EI and PPS-Pro?

The engine is method-agnostic. It tracks levels, ratios, and doses for any fertilizer routine, whether you follow EI (Estimative Index), PPS-Pro, lean dosing, or the GEN ratio method. GEN's N:K and Redfield targets are built in, and you can set your own target ranges for every element.

What tests do I need?

Ilaequor doesn't force you to use any specific tests. You can set up what elements you want to track, and if you want, you can set up a timer for every test you use.

Which platforms will it be available on?

First we will develop the web application, running only in browsers. But in the future we will create dedicated Android and iOS applications.

Will it be free?

The pricing is not set yet, but we are planning to offer a free plan.

Will the results of the tests be stored?

Yes, we will include a log functionality, so you can see how the situation evolves in time.

How is it different from an aquarium log app?

A log records what you type. Ilaequor also models what happens between tests to make a prediction and recommendation into the future.

What happens to my data?

Ilaequor stores your tank setup and your readings so it can show history and compute recommendations. You can export or delete your data at any time.
