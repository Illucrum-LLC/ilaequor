01 / The problem

Testing a planted tank shouldn't mean juggling timers with wet hands.

Reagents keep developing while you are still adding the next one, so you end up watching three timers with wet hands. Afterwards you re-derive doses in a spreadsheet, chase a single nitrate number, and guess at potassium you cannot reliably test. Two weeks later, you are not sure last time's dose moved anything at all.

Ilaequor closes that loop. It runs the timers, models where the tank really sits, and turns each session into your next set of doses.

02 / How it works

How Ilaequor works

Set the tank up once. After that, every two-week session follows the same four steps.

  1. Set up your tank

    Enter your net volume, your RO (reverse osmosis) water-change schedule, and the fertilizer stocks you dose. You do this once.

  2. Run the session

    At the tank, tap a test to start its countdown. Run several at once, in any order; the app follows your pace, not a fixed sequence.

  3. Enter each reading

    As each test develops, type the value. Ilaequor shows it in context: above or below target, better or worse than last session, and a rough ETA to target.

  4. Get your next doses

    Review the tank's modeled state and its ratios, elemental N:K (nitrogen to potassium) and the NO3:PO4 Redfield band (nitrate to phosphate), then accept or reject the next-period dose recommendations in grams and millilitres.

03 / What you get

What you get

  • One-handed session timers.

    A two-week test run stops being a two-hands-and-a-kitchen-timer job. Start a test, let it count down, keep working.

  • A modeled running state.

    Ilaequor dilutes for your water changes and adds what you dosed, so you know roughly where the tank sits between tests, including the potassium you cannot test reliably.

  • Ratios, not single readings.

    It tracks elemental N:K and the NO3:PO4 Redfield band against their target ranges, so you manage the balance the method actually cares about, not one nitrate number in isolation.

  • Doses from your own fertilizers.

    Recommendations come in grams and millilitres, computed from your own stock recipes and tank volume. The same math as your spreadsheet, without the spreadsheet.

  • A logbook that replaces the spreadsheet.

    Every session is saved, with history and trends you can scan across weeks.

04 / Who it's for

Who it's for

For you if

  • you run a freshwater planted tank

05 / FAQ

Common questions

Do I have to switch fertilizers to use Ilaequor?

No. Ilaequor works from the fertilizers you already dose. You enter each stock once, the fertilizer, the grams, and the volume of water you dissolved it in, and the app does the dosing math from your tank volume. There is nothing new to buy and no product line to switch to.

Which dosing methods does it support?

The engine is method-agnostic. It tracks levels, ratios, and doses for any fertilizer routine, whether you follow EI (Estimative Index), PPS-Pro, lean dosing, or the GEN ratio method. GEN's N:K and Redfield targets are built in, and you can set your own target ranges.

How is this different from a dosing calculator like Rotala Butterfly?

A calculator gives you one dose in isolation, from numbers you type in that moment. Ilaequor tracks your tank's measured state over time and recommends the next doses based on where the tank actually sits, so the numbers follow your real tank between tests rather than a generic target.
